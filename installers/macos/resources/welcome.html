⚡ Magic Click 2.0

AI-powered vision system — One-click installer

📋 What This Installer Does

Installs Magic Click to /Applications/MagicClick and automatically sets up all AI packages and models in a private environment. Your existing software is not affected.

🌐 Internet Required for First-Time Setup

The installer downloads AI models (~500 MB) from the internet. Please connect to Wi-Fi before clicking Install.
After the first setup, Magic Click works offline.

⏱ Estimated Setup Time

First run: 5–15 minutes depending on your internet speed.
All subsequent launches: under 10 seconds.

🔒 macOS Security Warning

If macOS shows "cannot be opened because the developer cannot be verified":

1
Right-click MagicClick.pkg (or the .app later) → Open
2
Click Open in the security dialog that appears
3
If the option doesn't appear: System Settings → Privacy & Security → scroll down → click Open Anyway
